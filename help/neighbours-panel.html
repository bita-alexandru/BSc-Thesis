Neighbours Panel

The Neighbours Panel is a 3x3 grid of buttons designed to define the neighbourhood of a cell.
From top-left to bottom-right, the buttons represent the following directions:

  • NW (North-West)
  • N (North)
  • NE (North-East)
  • W (West)
  • C (Center)
  • E (East)
  • SW (South-West)
  • S (South)
  • SE (South-East)

Enabling a button means including the cell placed at the immediate vicinity on the corresponding direction of the currently evaluated cell into its neighbourhood.

Disabling a button means excluding the cell placed at the immediate vicinity on the corresponding direction of the currently evaluated cell into its neighbourhood.
